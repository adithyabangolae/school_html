How can your company manage non-hazardous waste?

In today's time, most solar companies only deal with plastic and glass. Metals such as Cadmium is not an issue anymore because only a small fraction of the solar panel is made of metal and comapanies have adpated to plastic and glass.

solution 1: Contacting displosal sites

Contacting disposal sistes such as Rublic services, advanced disposal services, clean Harbors and other siposal sites can help your company by managing it's non-hazardous waste which is mostly composed of plastic and glass.They will have the facilities to help manage your companies waste.

solution 2: recycling your waste

Recycling is another way of managing plastic and glass and it brings many benefits. You can reuse the plastic and glass to make other solar panels. The broken glass piceses can be melted and remade into glass which can be used for making solar panels.This can be done by the disposal sites.

There are two types of plastic, plastic that can be recycled and plastic that can't be recycled. If you want your product to be completely renewable, then your product should consist of recyclable plastic. A furnace can be used to burn the plastic and resused to make new solar panels.
